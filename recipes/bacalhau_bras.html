Bacalhau à Bras

Bacalhau à Bras is a traditional Portuguese dish made with shredded salted cod, onions, and thinly chopped fried potatoes, all bound together with scrambled eggs. It’s a comforting and flavorful meal that’s perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 500g salted cod (bacalhau), soaked and shredded
  • 4 medium potatoes, peeled and cut into thin matchsticks
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 large eggs
  • Olive oil for frying
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

    • Trust the process :

    1. Start by soaking the salted cod in water for 24-48 hours, changing the water every 8 hours to remove excess salt. Once soaked, drain and shred the cod into small pieces.
    2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced onions and minced garlic, sautéing until they become soft and translucent.
    3. Add the shredded cod to the skillet and cook for a few minutes until it’s heated through. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    4. In a separate pan, heat more olive oil and fry the potato matchsticks until they are golden and crispy. Drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
    5. In a bowl, beat the eggs and season with a little salt and pepper. Add the fried potatoes to the skillet with the cod mixture, then pour the beaten eggs over everything. Stir gently until the eggs are just set but still creamy.
    6. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley before serving. Enjoy your Bacalhau à Bras!
