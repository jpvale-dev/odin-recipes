Francesinha

The Francesinha is a famous Portuguese sandwich originating from Porto


Before we cook together, let me tell you a little about it. Francesinha means “Little Frenchie.” But between you and me, the only small thing about it is the name. What I serve is a glorious layered sandwich made with:

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Steak (most important piece of the sandwich)
  • Ham
  • Two different sausages
  • Lots of melted cheese
  • A rich, slighty spicy sauce

    • Trust the process :

    1. Start by preparing the sauce. It’s a blend of tomatoes, beer, and spices that gives the Francesinha its signature flavor.
    2. Next, we’ll layer our sandwich. Start with a slice of bread, then add steak, ham, and sausages. Top it all off with another slice of bread.
    3. Now comes the cheesy part! Cover the sandwich with a generous amount of cheese and pop it in the oven until it’s melted and bubbly.
    4. Finally, pour that delicious sauce over the top and serve it up with some crispy fries on the side. Enjoy!
